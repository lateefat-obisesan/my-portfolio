Chocolate cake

Rich and moist chocolate cake topped with chocolate syrup.

Ingredient
Quantity Ingredient Instruction
1½ cups All purpose flour Sifted
½ cup Cocoa powder Sifted
1 cup Sugar Granulated
1½ tsp Baking powder Sifted
2 Eggs Whisked
1 cup Milk
½ cup Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Add eggs, milk, and butter.
  3. Pour into baking pan.
  4. Bake at 180°C(350°F) for 35 minutes.
  5. Let cool and add chocolate syrup.
