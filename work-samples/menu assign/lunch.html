Jollof rice

Nigerian jollof rice cooked in a rich bleanded tomato and pepper sauce, served with spicy peppered chicken.

Ingredient
Quantity Ingredient Instruction
2 cups Long-grain rice Washed & drained
3 tbsp Tomato paste
2 pieces Fresh tomato Blended
3 pieces Red bell pepper Blended
2 pieces Scotch bonnet pepper Blended
1 Onion Blended
¼ cup Groundnut oil
½ tsp Salt
⅙ tsp Spices

Instructions

  1. Blend fresh tomato, red bell pepper, scotch bonnet pepper, and onio until smooth.
  2. Heat groundnut oil in a pot and fry the tomato paste.
  3. Add the blended mixture and cook until thick and reduced.
  4. Season with salt and spices.
  5. Add washed rice and stock, the stir well.
  6. Cover and cook until the rice is soft and well cooked.
  7. Serve with peppered chicken on the side.
