Grilled Chicken & Roasted Veggies

Light and healthy grilled chicken served with oven-roasted vegetables.

Ingredient
Quantity Ingredient Instruction
2 pieces Chicken breast Cubed
2 Bell pepper Slice
2 Carrots Diced
2 tbsp Olive oil For frying
½ tbsp Salt
½ tbsp Pepper

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill until fully cooked.
  3. Chop vegetables and toss with oil.
  4. Roast vegetablesin the oven
  5. Serve together
