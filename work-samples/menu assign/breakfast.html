Pancakes

Fluffy homemade pancakes served warm with a dizzle of mable syrup. A perfect way to start your morning.

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient Instruction
1 cup All purpose flour Sifted
2 tsp Baking powder
2 tbsp Sugar
½ tsp Salt
1 cup Milk Room temp
1 Egg Lightly beaten
2 tsp Butter Melted
For serving Maple syrup

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder,sugar, and salt.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan on medium heat and lightly grease it.
  4. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with syrup on top.
