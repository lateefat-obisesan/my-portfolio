About LaPrake Ro Cafe

LaPrake Ro Cafe was born from the combined vision of four founders who believed that a coffee shop should be more than just a place to grab a caffeine fix. We wanted to create a space where the aroma of freshly roasted beans meets the warmth of genuine human connection. Each letter of "LaPrake Ro" represents the spirit of our founding team, dedicated to bringing you the finest brews in the city.

Our beans are ethically sourced and roasted in small batches to ensure that every cup tells a story of quality and care. Whether you are here for a quick espresso or a long afternoon of conversation, we strive to make every visit the highlight of your day. Thank you for being a part of our journey.

The Vision of the Founding Four

LaPrake Ro Cafe was born in 2022 when four friends: Lateefat, Prabhjot, Blake and Roop decided that coffee culture had lost its soul. We didn't want another "fast food" coffee chain. We wanted a sanctuary. Our name is a tribute to our partnership, representing the roaster, the designer, the baker, and the community builder who made this space possible. One of our founders is a master baker who ensures that our artisan pastries are made from scratch every morning to pair perfectly with our roasts.

We believe in the "Right" cup, not the "Fast" cup. From sourcing Grade 1 Arabica beans to engineering the perfect water temperature, every detail matters. We are not just a cafe; we are a social responsibility project focused on sustainability and human connection. When you sit at our hand-crafted wooden tables, you aren't just a customer; you're part of our family legacy.

Our Goal

To reduce coffee waste by 50% by 2027 through sustainable composting and circular packaging.

Our Mission

To ensure every bean is traced back to a farmer who was paid a fair, living wage, creating a transparent global supply chain.
