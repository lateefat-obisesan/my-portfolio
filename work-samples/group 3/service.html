Our Services

Wholesale Supply

We partner with local offices, boutique hotels, and fine-dining establishments to elevate their coffee programs. Our wholesale service isn't just a delivery; its a commitment to quality. We provide custom roast profiles tailored to your brand, ensuring that your clients experience the same excellence we serve in our own cafe.

Private Catering

Transform your event with our mobile specialty coffee bar. Whether its a wedding or a corporate gala, our professional baristas bring the full LaPrake Ro experience to you. We serve a full menu of espresso-based drinks, all featuring our hand-roasted beans. We handle everything from setup to cleanup, leaving you free to enjoy your guests.

Space Rental

Our minimalist industrial space is more than a cafe; its a hub for creators. After hours, we open our doors for private events, photography workshops, and creative meetups. With high-speed fiber internet, professional lighting, and an atmosphere designed for focus. Our team is available to provide drink service during your rental period.

Home Brewing Classes

Unlock the secrets of the perfect cup in our immersive brewing workshops. Led by our head baristas, these classes cover the science behind extraction. You will learn the 'Art of the Pour' using V60 and Chemex methods, understand the importance of water chemistry, and master the grind settings for different beans.
